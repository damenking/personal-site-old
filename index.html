About Me

I am a software developer with experience building web apps using JavaScript and Python. I received my BS in Business Administration at Portland State University with a focus on Management Information Systems. Since then I've had a variety of roles within the tech industry including business analyst, support technician, software trainer and project manager. I decided to move into software development in 2016 and have since been building my skills and experience in the web development field.
